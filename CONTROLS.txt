TOOMANYMACHINES — CONTROLS
==========================

DRIVING
  W / S            throttle / reverse
  A / D            steer
  Left Shift       boost (sprint on the walkers)
  Space            e-brake (cars only) — grip collapses, handbrake drift
  Mouse            aim (custom reticle)
  Left mouse       fire the selected weapon (hold for the mounted hardware)
  Right mouse      the GIANT LASER (the Colossus burns it far bigger)
  Backspace        restart
  Escape           quit

THE GARAGE — 1..9 pick the ride (Tab cycles)
  1  TRUCK        the classic — quick, drifty, dependable
  2  BUGGY        light gun-car, snappy handling
  3  SPORT        drift missile — fastest wheels, loosest tail
  4  APC          heavy plow — shrugs the crowd off
  5  TANK         the huge tank — slow, planted, heavy gun cadence
  6  GUNMECH      nimble walker battery
  7  MECH         the big walker — twin shoulder guns
  8  COLOSSUS     absolutely massive six-legged mech — HUGE laser
  9  WING         you fly: over the buildings, over the horde

BUILD (variant of the current ride) — F G H J K L
  F  standard
  G  scout        smaller, faster
  H  heavy        bigger, slower, harder cadence
  J  racer        +55% speed
  K  colossal     1.6x size, slow, big cadence
  L  prototype    rapid fire

WEAPONS — top row: patterns of the detonating shell
  Q  CANNON       one shell, detonates exactly at the cursor
  E  AUTO         rapid shells with spray
  R  BURST        3-shell fan
  T  RAIL         near-hitscan slug
  Y  MORTAR       5 shells scattered on the cursor
  U  LANCE        LMB holds the giant laser
  I  NOVA         defensive ring blast around the rig
  O  WALL         area-denial line at the cursor
  P  AIRSTRIKE    a stick of shells sweeping in along the aim line

WEAPONS — bottom row: MOUNTED hardware (hold LMB)
  Z  SINGULARITY  hold: a collapsing well REELS the horde into the cursor,
                  charging up — release (or top out) and the point ERUPTS
                  into a massive ring of detonations
  X  TWIN BEAMS   giant mounted lasers off both flanks — double kill lane
  C  SCYTHE       a laser blade sweeping an arc around the aim — mows crowds
  V  FLAMER       rolling fire cone; burns hard up close and shoves back
  B  ARC FIELD    crackling discharge around the rig — peel off a swarm
  N  VORTEX       drags the horde into the cursor (no damage — herd them
                  into your mines/shells/army)
  M  MINES        drops proximity mines off the tail; they arm after a
                  beat and BOOM when the horde steps close

YOUR ARMY (autonomous — it rebuilds at the MACHINE WORKS when it falls)
  Tank / gun-car / gunner-mech columns march out the avenues (the main
  street east of the pit gets the biggest push) and pour fire on whatever
  they lock. Suicide drones and bombers stage at the CENTER and sortie to
  wherever YOU are fighting, coming down on the horde as real blasts.
  Fallen allies leave wrecks — the salvage fleet feeds them to the pit.

THE FACTORIES (two pads flanking the pit on the first ring road — park
  ON a pad, or CLICK a pad on screen, and its panels open on the left;
  the scrap ledger + the fleet's income per minute sit in the top-left
  corner. Scrap = pit deposits the salvage fleet banks, exchanged 8:1
  — and EVERY BUILD COSTS SCRAP: the opening credit floats a full army
  of any fit, income comfortably covers rebuilding it, and the surplus
  is what buys upgrades)

  THE TECH LADDER: day one you own the GUN-CAR line and GATLING
    research — everything else shows LOCKED and is FOUNDED one by one
    with scrap: machines (gunner mech 250 / gun drone 500 / tank 800 /
    kamikaze 1500 / bomber 2500), weapons (cannon 300 / laser 800 /
    arc 1500), and each chassis' extra hardpoints (250 / 1000). Until
    a machine is founded the line builds gun-cars in its place, so
    every founding visibly adds its column to the army. Levels now
    climb to 7.

  MACHINE WORKS (east, on the main street crossing)
    CLICK a machine (or keys 1..6) to set what the line builds: TANK /
    GUN-CAR / GUNNER MECH / KAMIKAZE / BOMBER / GUN DRONE — every
    fallen ally is rebuilt as it. AUTO MIX = the classic column.
    Clicking an owned machine also opens its FIT panel:
      HARDPOINTS  big chassis carry more (tank 3, gunner mech 2,
                  gun-car 1, gun drone 2; kamikazes and bombers ARE
                  the payload). Click a hardpoint to cycle the gun on
                  it: CANNON / GATLING / LASER / ARC FIELD / EMPTY;
                  click a LOCKED one to open it.
      WEIGHT      every gun has weight; the bar shows load vs what the
                  frame carries. Upgrading the machine (+2 per level)
                  lets it lift heavier fits.
      BUILD COST  chassis price + the weight of everything mounted,
                  paid in scrap for every machine that rolls off.
    CLICK the buy button (or F): UNLOCK THE LINE while locked, then
    build levels — tougher hull, faster drive, more carry weight, a
    bigger chassis.

  WEAPONS LAB (west, mirrored across the pit)
    Weapon research: CLICK a weapon (or keys 1..4), RESEARCH it if
    locked, then buy levels — every hardpoint carrying it hits harder,
    on every machine, from the next build on.

  THE GUN DRONE: a flying quad-rotor weapons platform — ignores the
  streets entirely, circles its prey like a gun-car, and fires
  whatever the works hung under it.

  While the cursor works a panel the guns hold their fire. The pad
  itself still shows the readout: center glyph = selection, green pips
  = level, the inner arc = scrap banked toward the next upgrade.
